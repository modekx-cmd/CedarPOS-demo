💵
Dual currency everywhere

📄
Professional PDF invoices
Clean, print-ready invoices with your logo, VAT breakdown, dual-currency pricing, and exchange rate footer.
💳
Debt tracking & Debt Hunter
Track every customer's outstanding balance, view unpaid invoices, and send WhatsApp payment reminders.
📦
Full inventory management
Products, categories, cost vs sale price, stock logs, restock orders, supplier tracking, and CSV import.
📊
P&L and tax reports
Revenue, COGS, gross profit, and margin per product. Full tax report by date range. Export for your accountant.
🌐
Arabic RTL interface
Complete Arabic right-to-left layout across every page, modal, and form. Switch in one tap.

Barcode scanning & printing

Multi-user with roles
Admin sees everything. Staff sees only what they need. Permissions enforced on every page, not just navigation.

100% offline
No internet required. Auto-backup every 10 minutes to C:/POSbackup. One-click restore anytime.
